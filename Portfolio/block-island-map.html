The elevation data provides critical insights into the topography of Block Island, helping in urban planning, flood risk assessment, and environmental conservation. The DEM and contour maps are essential tools for understanding the island's terrain.

Points of Interest on Block Island

Northern Light

Built in 1867, this granite lighthouse sits on Sandy Point.

Southeast Light

Gothic-style lighthouse constructed in 1875, moved back from eroding bluffs in 1993.

The Great Salt Pond

This is the main body of water within Block Island and acts as a natural harbor for the inhabitants of Block Island.
